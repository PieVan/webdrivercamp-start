EMACs

0 - C-x C-f // open
1 - C-x C-s // save
2 - C-a C-k //cut entire one
3 - C-y //paste
4 - C-s //search
5 - C-/ or C-x //undo
6 - C-x C-c //exit/quite

10 - u //undo

12 - 0 or ^ //move to the begining of the line
13 - $ //move to the end of the line
